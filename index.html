What does it mean to be an independent agent?
+
An independent agent isn't locked to a single insurance company. We work with 30+ carriers and shop your policy to all of them simultaneously. That competition drives your price down — unlike a captive agent who can only offer one company's rates.
Is Florida coastal / windstorm insurance hard to find?
+
It can be — many national carriers have pulled back from Florida's coastal market. But we specialize exactly here. We have carrier relationships for high-wind zones, coastal properties, and difficult-to-insure homes that other agents struggle to place.
How fast can I get a quote?
+
Most standard quotes come back same business day. For complex commercial or coastal properties, usually within 24-48 hours. If you have a closing deadline, tell us upfront — we can accommodate rush requests. Uploading a 4-point or wind mitigation report bumps you to priority.
Do I need flood insurance in Florida?
+
If you're in a FEMA-designated high-risk flood zone with a mortgage, it's typically required. But even outside high-risk zones, Florida's geography means flood risk is real — and homeowners insurance never covers flood damage. We compare NFIP vs. private flood to find the best deal.
Can you help mortgage officers and realtors?
+
Absolutely — this is a core part of what we do. We communicate directly with lenders, provide binders and dec pages fast, and make sure nothing holds up a closing. We'd love to be your go-to insurance resource for your clients.
What counties do you not serve?
+
We currently serve 66 of 67 Florida counties. The only exception is Manatee County. If you're anywhere else in Florida, we are ready to help.
